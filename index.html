About Charmers

What began as a small community project in Tennessee has grown into a trusted brand for custom jewelry.

Rooted in Craftsmanship, Built on Trust

Charmers was founded and is operated in Centerville, Tennessee, with a focus on creating beautiful, personalized accessories that tell your story. We believe in transparent processes, secure transactions, and quality that lasts.

Our journey started with a simple bracelet project for our local community and has evolved into a structured business that maintains its small-town values while embracing modern technology.

Charmers partners with Montessori School of Franklin

Charmers has joined forces with the Montessori School of Franklin, Tennessee to launch Little Charmers™, a student-driven initiative designed to bring Charmers bracelets directly to students at discounted rates.
